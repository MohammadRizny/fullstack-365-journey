Image in HTML

HTML (HyperText Markup Language) is the standard language used to create and structure content on the web. It defines the layout of a webpage by using elements such as headings, paragraphs, links, images, lists, tables, and forms. HTML works as the backbone of every website, allowing browsers to understand and display content correctly. It is easy to learn, beginner-friendly, and essential for anyone starting web development, as it provides the foundation needed to add styling with CSS and interactivity with JavaScript.

I like to visit some where.

  1. Caribbean

    The Caribbean is famous for its stunning beaches, crystal-clear turquoise waters, and warm tropical climate that attracts visitors from around the world. Soft white sand shores, swaying palm trees, and vibrant cora reefs create a perfect setting for relaxation and adventure. Caribbean beaches are ideal for swimming, snorkeling, sunbathing, and enjoying breathtaking sunsets, while the laid-back island culture adds to the charm. With its natural beauty and peaceful atmosphere, the Caribbean offers an unforgettable beach experience.

  2. Paris

    The Eiffel Tower is the most iconic landmark in Paris and a symbol of France’s rich history and culture. Standing tall along the Seine River, it offers breathtaking views of the city and attracts millions of visitors each year. Paris, often called the “City of Love,” is known for its elegant architecture, art, fashion, and romantic atmosphere. Together, the Eiffel Tower and the beautiful streets of Paris represent timeless charm, making the city one of the most admired destinations in the world.
